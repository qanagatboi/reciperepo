Vegetarian Ramen

Vegetarian Ramen

Rustle up this warming veggie ramen in just 15 minutes. Think of it as healthy fast food
it's low-fat, low-calorie and low-cost as well

    Ingredients:
  • 80g pack instant noodles
  • It will be more authetic if you find real Asian noodles.
  • 2 spring onions.finell choped
  • 1/2 head pak choi
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • chilli sauce(optional)
    Cooking Order:
      Step 1
    1. Cook the noodles with the using of sachet of flavouring provided
      • If you have stock at home, use it instead of sachet
    2. Add the spring onions and pak choi at for the final min.
      Step 2
    1. Simmer the egg for 6 min after boiling
    2. Run it under the cold water to stop it cooking and then peel it
    3. Toast the sesame seeds in a frying pan
      Step 3
    1. Tip the noodle and greens into a deep bowl
    2. Halve the boiled egg and place it on top
    3. Sprinkle with sesame seeds
    4. Drizzle the sause or sesemae oil provided
